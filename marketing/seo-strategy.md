### Blog SEO Template

Every blog post should follow this structure:

**Title Tag Formula**: [Primary Keyword]: [Benefit/Number] ([Year if relevant])
- Example: "Tarif Plombier : Comment Fixer Vos Prix (Guide 2025)"

**URL Structure**: /blog/[primary-keyword-slug]
- Example: /blog/tarif-plombier-guide-complet

**Content Structure**:
1. **Introduction** (100-150 words)
   - Hook with primary keyword in first sentence
   - What reader will learn
   - Why it matters

2. **Table of Contents** (for 2000+ word articles)
   - Improves UX and generates jump links

3. **Main Content**
   - H2 and H3 subheadings every 300-400 words
   - Primary keyword in at least one H2
   - Secondary keywords in H3s
   - Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
   - Bullet points and lists

4. **Visual Elements**
   - At least 3-5 images/screenshots
   - Alt text with keywords
   - Charts, infographics, tables

5. **Key Takeaways Section**
   - Bullet summary of main points

6. **FAQ Section**
   - 5-10 common questions
   - FAQ schema markup for rich snippets

7. **CTA Section**
   - Related PlombiPro feature
   - Trial signup or lead magnet

**Internal Linking**:
- Link to 3-5 related blog posts
- Link to relevant landing pages (features, pricing)
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

### Review Generation & Management

**Strategy**:
1. **Ask for Reviews** from happy customers
   - Timing: After successful onboarding or positive interaction
   - Method: Email with direct Google review link
   - Incentive: None (against Google policy), but can offer appreciation

2. **Make it Easy**
   - Direct link: g.page/[your-business-name]/review
   - QR code for in-person requests
   - Email template with simple instructions

3. **Respond to All Reviews**
   - Positive: Thank them, highlight specific points they mentioned
   - Negative: Apologize, offer to resolve privately, show you care
   - Response time: Within 48 hours

**Review Monitoring**:
- Google Alerts for brand name
- Weekly check of Google My Business
- Track average rating over time (target: 4.5+)

## Risk Mitigation

### Algorithm Updates
- **Risk**: Google algorithm changes could impact rankings
- **Mitigation**: Focus on quality content, user experience, E-A-T principles
- **Monitoring**: Stay updated via SEO news sites, Google announcements

### Competitive Pressure
- **Risk**: Competitors increase SEO investment
- **Mitigation**: Differentiate with unique content, Factur-X angle, niche focus
- **Monitoring**: Quarterly competitive analysis

### Content Quality Issues
- **Risk**: Thin, duplicate, or low-quality content hurts rankings
- **Mitigation**: Editorial standards, expert review, plagiarism checks
- **Monitoring**: Regular content audits

### Technical Issues
- **Risk**: Site downtime, slow speeds, broken pages
- **Mitigation**: Monitoring tools, regular technical audits, Cloudflare CDN
- **Monitoring**: Uptime monitoring (Pingdom, UptimeRobot)
