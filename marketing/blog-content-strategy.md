## Content Strategy Overview

### Goals
1. **SEO**: Rank for high-intent keywords plumbers search for
2. **Education**: Establish PlombiPro as industry thought leader
3. **Conversion**: Drive trial signups through valuable content
4. **Retention**: Keep existing users engaged with tips and updates

### Publishing Frequency
- **Target**: 2-3 articles per week (8-12/month)
- **Minimum**: 1 article per week (4/month)
- **Mix**: 60% educational, 25% how-to, 15% industry news/updates

### Content Types
1. **Ultimate Guides** (3000-5000 words) - Comprehensive, evergreen SEO content
2. **How-To Tutorials** (1500-2500 words) - Practical, actionable advice
3. **Industry News & Analysis** (800-1500 words) - Timely, relevant updates
4. **Case Studies** (1200-2000 words) - Customer success stories
5. **Listicles** (1000-1800 words) - Easy-to-scan, shareable content
6. **Tool Comparisons** (1500-2500 words) - Product comparison content

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Professional but approachable**: You're a peer, not a professor
- **Practical and actionable**: Every article should have takeaways
- **French-focused**: Examples, regulations, prices specific to France
- **No jargon overload**: Explain technical terms simply

### Structure
1. **Hook introduction** (100-150 words): Why this matters, what they'll learn
2. **Table of contents** (for long articles 2000+ words)
3. **Short paragraphs** (2-4 sentences max)
4. **Subheadings every 300-400 words** (H2, H3)
5. **Bullet points and lists** (easier to scan)
6. **Visual elements** (images, infographics, screenshots)
7. **Key takeaways box** (summary at the end)
8. **Clear CTA** (try PlombiPro, download template, etc.)

### SEO Best Practices
- **Primary keyword in**: Title, URL, first paragraph, H2, conclusion
- **Secondary keywords**: Sprinkled naturally throughout
- **Internal links**: Link to 2-3 other blog posts
- **External links**: 1-2 authoritative sources
- **Alt text on images**: Descriptive with keywords
- **Meta description**: 150-160 characters, compelling with keyword

### Content Upgrades (Lead Magnets)
Each major article should have a downloadable content upgrade:
- Templates (quote, invoice, email templates)
- Checklists (installation checklist, compliance checklist)
- Calculators (pricing calculator, margin calculator)
- Guides (PDF versions of long articles)

## SEO Optimization Checklist

### Before Writing
- [ ] Keyword research completed
- [ ] Search intent identified (informational, commercial, transactional)
- [ ] Competitor analysis done (top 3 ranking articles reviewed)
- [ ] Outline with H2/H3 structure created

### During Writing
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (ideally at the beginning)
- [ ] Primary keyword in URL slug
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Secondary keywords used naturally
- [ ] Readability score checked (Hemingway, Yoast)
- [ ] Internal links to 2-3 relevant articles
- [ ] External links to 1-2 authoritative sources

### After Writing
- [ ] Title tag optimized (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Meta description written (150-160 characters)
- [ ] Images added with descriptive file names
- [ ] Alt text added to all images
- [ ] FAQ schema markup added (if applicable)
- [ ] Article schema markup added
- [ ] Mobile-friendly format checked
- [ ] Page speed optimized

### Post-Publication
- [ ] Submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] Shared on social media
- [ ] Emailed to relevant subscriber segments
- [ ] Promoted in relevant Facebook groups
- [ ] Monitor rankings and traffic
- [ ] Update and refresh after 6-12 months

## Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics
1. **Organic traffic**: Sessions from Google search
2. **Keyword rankings**: Position for target keywords
3. **Engagement**: Time on page, scroll depth, bounce rate
4. **Conversions**: Trial signups from blog traffic
5. **Backlinks**: Number and quality of external links

### Tools
- **Google Analytics**: Traffic and engagement
- **Google Search Console**: Rankings and click-through rates
- **SEMrush/Ahrefs**: Keyword tracking and competitor analysis
- **Hotjar**: User behavior and heatmaps
- **PlombiPro internal tracking**: Signups attributed to blog

### Monthly Review Process
1. Identify top 10 performing articles (traffic, conversions)
2. Identify bottom 10 performing articles (analyze why)
3. Update/refresh 2-3 older articles with new information
4. Identify keyword opportunities (keywords we rank 11-20 for)
5. Plan next month's content based on performance
