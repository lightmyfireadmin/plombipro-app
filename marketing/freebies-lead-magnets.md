## Lead Magnet Strategy

### Purpose of Lead Magnets
1. **Email List Building**: Grow subscriber base for email marketing
2. **Lead Qualification**: Identify serious prospects vs browsers
3. **Value Demonstration**: Show PlombiPro's expertise before trial
4. **SEO Content Upgrades**: Enhance blog posts with downloadables
5. **Retargeting Assets**: Bring back website visitors

### Conversion Funnel Integration
```
Awareness → Lead Magnet Download → Email Nurture → Free Trial → Paid Customer
```

### Requirements for Effective Lead Magnets
✅ **Immediately useful**: Solve a specific problem now
✅ **Quick to consume**: 5-15 minutes max to get value
✅ **High perceived value**: Looks professional, saves time/money
✅ **Relevant to product**: Natural bridge to PlombiPro trial
✅ **Easy to access**: Simple email exchange, instant download

### Landing Page Template Structure

Every lead magnet landing page should include:

1. **Hero Section**:
   - Compelling headline (benefit-driven)
   - Subheadline (expand on the benefit)
   - Hero image (mockup of the lead magnet)
   - Email capture form (name + email only)

2. **What's Included Section**:
   - Bullet list of contents
   - Visual preview (screenshots/samples)
   - File format and size information

3. **Who This Is For**:
   - 3-4 specific use cases
   - Problems it solves

4. **Social Proof**:
   - Number of downloads
   - Testimonials from users (if available)
   - Trust badges

5. **FAQ Section**:
   - "Is it really free?" - Yes, no credit card required
   - "What happens after I download?" - Explain email sequence
   - "Will you spam me?" - No, only valuable content

6. **Final CTA**:
   - Repeat email form
   - Benefit-driven button text ("Télécharger Maintenant")

## Design & Branding Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Professional but approachable**: Not corporate, not amateur
- **Clean and scannable**: Lots of white space, clear hierarchy
- **Branded**: PlombiPro logo, brand colors, consistent fonts
- **High-quality images**: Professional photos or illustrations, no cheesy stock photos

### File Specifications
- **PDFs**: Optimized for web (small file size), printer-friendly
- **Excel/Word**: Compatible with older versions (2016+)
- **Images**: High resolution (300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web)
- **File naming**: Descriptive, no spaces (e.g., plombipro-modele-devis-2025.pdf)

### Accessibility
- [ ] Text is readable (minimum 12pt font)
- [ ] High contrast (dark text on light background)
- [ ] Hyperlinks are underlined or clearly distinguished
- [ ] PDFs are searchable (not scanned images)
- [ ] Alt text on images (if digital versions)
