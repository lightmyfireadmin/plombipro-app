## Email Marketing Strategy Overview

### Goals
1. **Lead Nurturing**: Convert website visitors to trial users (10-15% conversion)
2. **Trial Activation**: Get trial users to experience core features (60%+ activation)
3. **Trial-to-Paid**: Convert active trial users to paying customers (20-30%)
4. **Customer Retention**: Keep customers engaged and prevent churn (<5% monthly churn)
5. **Expansion**: Upsell free/starter users to higher plans (10-15% upgrade rate)

### Email List Segments

| Segment | Definition | Size Estimate | Primary Goal |
|---------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Cold Leads** | Downloaded lead magnet, not yet trial | Growing | Trial signup |
| **Trial Users (Day 1-14)** | Active free trial | 50-200/month | Activation & conversion |
| **Free Plan Users** | On permanent free plan | 100-300 | Engagement & upgrade |
| **Paid Customers (Starter)** | â‚¬15/month plan | 50-150 | Retention & upsell |
| **Paid Customers (Pro)** | â‚¬29/month plan | 30-100 | Retention & advocacy |
| **Churned Users** | Cancelled within 90 days | Varies | Win-back |
| **Inactive Users** | No login in 30+ days | Varies | Re-engagement |

### Email Types & Frequency

| Email Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Welcome/Onboarding** | Triggered | First impressions, set expectations |
| **Educational/Tips** | 2x/week for leads, 1x/week for customers | Value delivery, engagement |
| **Feature Announcements** | As needed (1-2x/month) | Product updates, excitement |
| **Promotional** | 1x/month max | Limited-time offers, urgency |
| **Transactional** | Triggered | Receipts, password resets, confirmations |
| **Re-engagement** | Triggered after inactivity | Prevent churn, win-back |

## Email Design & Technical Setup

### Email Service Provider
**Recommendation**: SendGrid or Resend (already integrated)

### Email Template Design

**Structure**:
- **Header**: PlombiPro logo, clear branding
- **Pre-header**: Compelling preview text (50-100 characters)
- **Body**: Single column, mobile-optimized
- **CTA Buttons**: Large, clear, contrasting color
- **Footer**: Unsubscribe link, company address, social links

**Design Guidelines**:
- Fonts: Sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica) for readability
- Colors: PlombiPro brand colors (blues, greens)
- Images: Minimal, optimized for fast loading
- Alt text: Always included for accessibility
- Width: Maximum 600px for desktop compatibility

---

### Personalization Tokens

Use throughout emails:
- `{{first_name}}` - First name
- `{{company_name}}` - Company name
- `{{plan_name}}` - Current plan (Free, Starter, Pro)
- `{{trial_end_date}}` - Trial expiration
- `{{days_left}}` - Days remaining in trial
- `{{last_login}}` - Last login date
- `{{quote_count}}` - Number of quotes created
- `{{invoice_count}}` - Number of invoices sent

---

### Deliverability Best Practices

**Technical Setup**:
- [ ] SPF record configured
- [ ] DKIM signing enabled
- [ ] DMARC policy set
- [ ] Dedicated IP address (once volume >50K emails/month)
- [ ] Warm-up period for new domain

**Content Best Practices**:
- Avoid spam trigger words ("Free", "Act now", "Limited time" - use sparingly)
- Good text-to-image ratio (more text than images)
- Include plain text version of every email
- Always include unsubscribe link (legal requirement)
- Consistent "From" name and email address

**List Hygiene**:
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Remove inactive users after 12 months
- Re-engagement campaign before removal
- Double opt-in for newsletter subscriptions

---
