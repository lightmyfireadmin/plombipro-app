## 10. RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

### 10.1 Key Risks

- **Likelihood**: Medium (30%)
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:

- **Likelihood**: Low (10%)

- **Mitigation**:

  - Alternative: Scraping (backup)
  - Alternative: Manual catalog updates

**Risk 3: Regulation Changes (Facturation 2026)**
- **Likelihood**: High (80% - already announced)
- **Impact**: Medium (table stakes)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Already building compliance

  - Partnerships with accounting software
  - Market as strength: "We're already compliant"

**Risk 4: Low Free-to-Paid Conversion**
- **Likelihood**: Medium (40%)
- **Impact**: High (business model fails)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Test: 5 devis limit vs 10 devis limit
  - Add: "Upgrade prompts" in app
  - Improve: Onboarding flow (show value fast)
  - Benchmark: Target 20% (SaaS freemium avg 2-5%)

**Risk 5: High Churn Rate**
- **Likelihood**: Medium (50%)
- **Impact**: High (growth cancels out)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Onboarding: Ensure first quote created in 48h
  - Support: Proactive outreach to inactive users
  - Product: Build sticky features (quote history)
  - Pricing: Annual plans (lower churn)

**Risk 6: Funding Runway Runs Out**
- **Likelihood**: Medium (depends on burn rate)
- **Impact**: Critical (shut down)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Bootstrap: Reach profitability on organic growth
  - Raise: Only if hit 150+ paying customers (traction)
  - Cut: Keep burn <â‚¬5k/month until â‚¬10k MRR

### 11.3 Decision-Making Framework

**When to Build vs Buy vs Partner:**

**Build:**
- Core differentiation (scanner OCR, catalog integration)
- Proprietary data or algorithms
- When better than market solutions

**Buy (SaaS):**
- Commoditized functions (email, analytics, CRM)
- When time-to-value is critical
- When expertise required (security, compliance)

**Partner:**
- Distribution channels (Point P, Cedeo)
- Complementary products (accounting software)
- When both parties benefit (co-marketing)

**Example Decisions:**
- Scanner OCR: **Build** (differentiation)
- Email marketing: **Buy** (Mailchimp)
- Catalog data: **Partner** (Point P API)
- Accounting export: **Partner** (integrate with Sage)
